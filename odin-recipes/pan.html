Pancakes With Fruits Recipe

Description:

Golden-brown pancakes adorned with a colorful assortment of fresh, juicy berries. This delightful combination of flavors offers a harmonious blend of sweetness, tartness, and textures, creating a mouthwatering treat that tantalizes the taste buds and brings joy to every bite.

Ingredients

  • - 2 cups flour
  • - 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • - 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • - ¼ teaspoon salt
  • - 2 tablespoon sugar
  • - 1¼ cups sour cream
  • - 1 cup milk
  • - 2 eggs
  • - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • - ½ cup blueberries
  • - ½ cup strawberries
  • - ½ cup rasberries

Steps

1. In a medium sized bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar. Set aside.

2. In a large mixing bowl whisk together the sour cream, milk, eggs and vanilla. Whisk together until everything is combined well.

3. Pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix with a whisk until it is just incorporated. It is ok to have lumps, do not over stir pancake batter.

4. Gently stir in the blueberries and strawberries with a spoon.

5. If using non stick, add enough batter directly on the pan to reach the desired size pancake. If not using non stick, melt a little butter on the griddle before adding the pancake batter.

6. Once the tops have small air bubbles forming, flip the pancake. Usually this is about 1-2 minutes but griddles and stoves vary so look for air bubbles.

7. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, until the bottoms are nicely browned.

8. Remove from heat and serve with fruits of your choice.
