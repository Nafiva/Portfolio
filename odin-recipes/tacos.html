Tacos Recipe

Description:

Steak Tacos are a tantalizing delight for the senses, these steak tacos showcase succulent grilled steak, perfectly seasoned and nestled in warm tortillas. With each bite, savor the mouthwatering fusion of tender meat, enhanced by a medley of fresh ingredients and zesty flavors that dance on your palate. Whether enjoyed with a squeeze of lime or a dollop of guacamole, these tacos offer a culinary experience that is both satisfying and unforgettable, bringing together the richness of steak and the vibrancy of Mexican-inspired seasonings in a symphony of taste.

Ingredients

  • - 2 limes, juice
  • - 1 tsp salt
  • - 1 tsp chili powder
  • - 1/2 tsp cumin
  • - 1/2 tsp paprika
  • - 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • - 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • - 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • - 1/4 tsp oregano
  • - 1.33 lbs. lean sirloin steak, trimmed of all fat
  • - 8 corn tortillas
  • - 1/4 cup cilantro
  • - 1/4 cup onions, chopped
  • - 8 corn tortillas
  • - 1/2 cup salsa

Steps

1. Preheat the oven to broil. You can also use a grill.

2. Combine the lime juice and spices. Rub over the steak.

3. Broil (or grill) for 5-6 minutes per side for medium-rare, or to your desired doneness. Let rest for 10 minutes and slice thin or chop into small pieces. You can also chop the steak into smaller pieces and cook it in a skillet. It will take 3-5 minutes in a hot skillet with about one tablespoon of oil.

4. Prepare the taco fillings. While the steak is resting, prepare your desired taco fillings. You can choose from options like saut√©ed onions and peppers, sliced avocado, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, grated cheese, and sour cream. Prepare these ingredients and arrange them in separate bowls for easy assembly.

5. Warm the tortillas and assemble the tacos. Heat a skillet over medium heat and warm the tortillas for about 30 seconds on each side until they become soft and pliable. As you warm the tortillas, transfer them to a plate and cover them with a clean kitchen towel to keep them warm. Once all the tortillas are warmed, assemble the tacos by placing a few slices or pieces of the cooked steak on each tortilla, followed by your desired fillings. Finish off with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro and any additional toppings you prefer.
